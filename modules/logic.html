4 · Report-specific logic

  • Bad-Lead Intelligence: keeps leads whose status is in the official Bad Lead category (Archive, Dead Lead, Spam, Video Estimate + “$” variants). Charts break down by status / source / rep / state / price / CF.
  • Hatch ↔ CRM: two-stage match of Hatch contacts to CRM — stage 1 exact name, stage 2 phone (last 10 digits) on the name-misses. A contact is “in CRM” if either matches. Angi audit = Angi leads not found in CRM, flagged by whether they’re in Hatch.
  • Communication: emails matched to leads by customer email address. Lead status = the Email Tracker sheet’s value, falling back to live moveboard by email when blank. Analyses: emails by status, per-customer frequency, totals by status, customers with no email, trend over time.
  • MVD Analysis: leads joined to calls & SMS by phone (last 10 digits). A call counts as connected if its result/action reads connected/accepted/answered and not missed/voicemail/busy/failed.
